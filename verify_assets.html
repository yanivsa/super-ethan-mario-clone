Ending Runtime Policy

Current runtime policy: player_win stays disabled until a dedicated high-quality end-state asset is explicitly approved in the manifest. Holiday goal visuals are currently procedural fallbacks rendered by the engine, and Ethan falls back to the standard runtime animation, until a dedicated ending asset pack is added. Missing ending art must never block gameplay.
